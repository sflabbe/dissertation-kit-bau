\section{Parameter Variations}
\label{sec:param_variations}

\subsection{Influence of Mass Ratio}
\label{subsec:mass_ratio}

Figure~\ref{fig:mass_ratio} shows the maximum response as a function of mass ratio $m_2/m_1$.

\begin{figure}[htbp]
	\centering
	\fbox{\parbox{0.8\textwidth}{\centering\vspace{5cm}[Response vs. mass ratio]\vspace{5cm}}}
	\caption{System response versus mass ratio}
	\label{fig:mass_ratio}
\end{figure}

Key findings:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Response increases with decreasing mass ratio
	\item Optimal ratio exists around 0.1--0.2
	\item Very small ratios lead to instability
\end{itemize}

\section{Summary}
\label{sec:sim_summary}

Parameter studies reveal:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Mass ratio is critical for system performance
	\item Damping effectively reduces peak responses
	\item Frequency characteristics determine resonance behavior
\end{enumerate}
